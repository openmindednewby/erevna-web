## FAQ

**Who is Erevna for?**
Teams that need to ask their audience or staff structured questions: educators running quizzes, HR running employee surveys, market researchers gathering customer feedback, event organizers collecting RSVPs.

**Do my respondents need an account?**
No. Share a link or QR code; respondents fill the form anonymously or with a one-tap login if you require it.

**Can I match my company's branding?**
Yes. Upload your logo, set your palette, and (on paid plans) choose your typography from a curated library. Every form your team builds inherits the look automatically.

**How does the free tier work?**
Free forever for one form and up to 100 responses per month. Respondents see a discreet "Powered by Erevna" line in the footer.

**Where does my data live?**
Your data, always. Export anytime as CSV or JSON. We don't sell or share respondent data with third parties. Hosted in the EU.

**Can I cancel anytime?**
Yes. Monthly billing, cancel from your account. No long-term contracts — we don't believe in lock-in for products like this.

## Voice notes (so you can edit consistently)

- **Direct, not breezy.** Erevna's audience includes corporate teams (Tag Heuer-tier). Avoid "✨ delightful" / emoji / startup-cute language.
- **Verb-led.** "Build forms" / "Translate" / "Match your brand" — not "Forms can be built" / "Translation is supported."
- **Short sentences, but not telegraphic.** 12–25 words is the sweet spot.
- **No competitor name-drops.** No "unlike Typeform" / "Google Forms killer." Implicit differentiation is more confident.
